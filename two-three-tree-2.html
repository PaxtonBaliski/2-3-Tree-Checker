About this tool
  • Implements 2-3 tree insert/delete from CMPT 280: split on insert, steal-left → steal-right → give-left → give-right priority on delete.
  • Internal nodes hold up to two keys (k1, k2); only leaves hold data items.
  • All leaves stay at the same level. The traversal panel walks left-to-right across the leaf chain.
  • Internal-node keys may not match any leaf-level item after deletions — this is expected.
